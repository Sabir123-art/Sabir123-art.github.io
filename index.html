Misconceptions About Vaccines

Rajeev Singh | Posted on 2021-05-20 Disqus:

In these tough times we are facing a lot of problem which is enormous in number. The pandemic has no solution other than Vaccines. So, why people fear This? Some people think is it safe so?
will it result cause in any other severe problems?….. So hold on guyss Read this full article and we assure you at the end you will get your answers.

So basically starting with “What are Vaccines and How do they really Work?”
A vaccine is a biological preparation that improves immunity to a particular disease caused by any virus or bacteria. Mostly vaccines contain parts of microbes or whole microbes that have been killed or weakened so that they don’t cause any disease.
When a person is Vaccinated, the immune system confronts these harmless versions of the germs. The immune system quickly clears them from the body.
In turn the body remembers the germ so that later if they attack again the retained memory help to fight against the particular germ and that’s how it work.

Starting with whether covid vaccines are safe or not, So from my point of view and considering those recommendatoions from W.H.O. these vaccines are the only first and last option we have right now.
These may cause you several mild-to-moderate side effects when receiving vaccinations. This is because your immune system is instructing your body to react in certain ways: it increases blood flow so more immune cells can circulate, and it raises your body temperature in order to kill the virus.

So bascially you may expose to some mild fever, headache or some body pain. And no such long term side effects shown yet mostly. Don’t fear within 10-14 days your immune system start to develop again and sooner you will be all normal again.
